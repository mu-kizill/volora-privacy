Privacy Policy – Volora

Effective Date: 09 March 2026

Volora is developed by Bleepsmind Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Volora mobile application.

1. Overview

Volora is designed as a calm, offline-first vocabulary learning application. The app does not require account creation and does not directly collect personally identifiable information.

2. Information Collection

Volora does not collect or store names, email addresses, phone numbers, or personal study data on external servers. All learning progress remains stored locally on your device.

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services

Volora uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service, to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including but not limited to:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interaction data

This information may be used for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes.

On iOS devices, users will be asked for permission to allow tracking in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. Users may choose to allow or deny tracking and can modify their choice at any time through their device settings.

For more information about how Google processes data, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Storage

All vocabulary progress and repetition data are stored locally on the user’s device. Volora does not transmit personal study data to external servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Volora is not directed at children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
